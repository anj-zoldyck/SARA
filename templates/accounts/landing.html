About S.A.R.A.

The Santa Rita Automated Relief Assistance System (S.A.R.A.) is a web-based, RFID-enabled platform designed to improve household profiling and ensure fair, transparent, and efficient distribution of relief assistance.

MSWDO Vision, Mission & Goals

Vision

The MSWDO envisions the Municipality of Santa Rita as a community where the vulnerable, marginalized and disadvantaged individuals and families are empowered through relevant and timely social protection, thereby improving their quality of life by 2025.

Mission

The MSWDO commits to effectively implement its social protection programs to all vulnerable, marginalized disadvantaged sectors of the municipality, significantly contributing to the achievement of poverty alleviation and people empowerment.

Goals
  • To plan and develop sound strategies for the effective implementation of social development programs and projects.
  • To provide integrated welfare packages that would benefit the marginalized and disadvantaged individuals and groups through the provision of care, protection and rehabilitation through effective social welfare assistance and social work interventions.
  • To have a network with government and non-government agencies for effective coordination to uplift the living conditions of target beneficiaries.
