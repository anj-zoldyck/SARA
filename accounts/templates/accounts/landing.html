About the System

What is S.A.R.A.?

The Santa Rita Automated Relief Assistance System (S.A.R.A.) is a web-based, RFID-enabled platform designed to improve household profiling and ensure fair, transparent, and efficient distribution of relief assistance to the families of Santa Rita, Pampanga.

Secure

RFID-verified beneficiary identification prevents fraudulent claims and duplicate distributions.

Transparent

Every distribution is recorded and fully traceable, ensuring accountability at all levels.

Efficient

Streamlined workflows reduce distribution time and administrative burden significantly.

Capabilities

Key Features

RFID Identification

Secure, contactless beneficiary verification using RFID cards issued to registered households.

Real-Time Dashboard

Live monitoring of aid distribution progress, scan logs, and beneficiary status across all barangays.

Household Profiling

Comprehensive digital profiling of households, families, and members down to zone level.

Report Generation

Generate official aid distribution reports for MSWDO review, auditing, and compliance purposes.

Distribution Scheduling

Plan and publish aid schedules accessible to all barangay administrators in real time.

Multi-Role Access

Role-based access for MSWDO administrators and barangay personnel with tailored permissions.

Process

How It Works

01

Register

Barangay staff collect and encode household and family member data into the system.

02

Assign RFID

Each registered family receives a unique RFID card linked to their household profile.

03

Distribute

RFID cards are scanned at distribution points to verify and record each claim instantly.

04

Track & Report

MSWDO monitors distribution progress and generates official reports for all aid programs.

Impact

Benefits & Impact

Accurate Distribution

RFID verification eliminates manual errors and ensures only eligible beneficiaries receive aid.

No Duplicate Claims

The system flags and prevents families from claiming aid multiple times per cycle.

Full Transparency

Every transaction is logged with timestamps, enabling complete audit trails for accountability.

Data-Driven Decisions

Analytics and reports empower MSWDO to plan more targeted and effective aid programs.

Faster Processing

Automated verification reduces queue times and speeds up distribution events significantly.

Reduced Paperwork

Digital records replace manual logbooks, reducing administrative workload at all levels.

MSWDO

Vision, Mission & Goals

Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office — Santa Rita, Pampanga

Vision

The MSWDO envisions the Municipality of Santa Rita as a community where the vulnerable, marginalized and disadvantaged individuals and families are empowered through relevant and timely social protection, thereby improving their quality of life by 2025.

Mission

The MSWDO commits to effectively implement its social protection programs to all vulnerable, marginalized and disadvantaged sectors of the municipality, significantly contributing to the achievement of poverty alleviation and people empowerment.

Goals

  • Plan and develop sound strategies for effective implementation of social development programs and projects.
  • Provide integrated welfare packages for marginalized individuals through care, protection and rehabilitation.
  • Network with government and non-government agencies for effective coordination to uplift beneficiaries.
